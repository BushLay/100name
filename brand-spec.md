# Name 100 Visual System

## Direction

- Anchor: Mailchimp Freddie-era warm humanism, adapted for Name 100.
- Product character: approachable knowledge game, playful without becoming childish.
- Mode: visual overhaul with routes, behavior, content, accessibility, and data contracts preserved.
- Do not reproduce Mailchimp trademarks, Freddie, or commissioned illustration assets.

## Assets

- Product logo: `public/logo.png`
- App icons: `app/icon.png`, `app/favicon.ico`
- Local type: `app/fonts/geist-sans-*.woff2`, `app/fonts/geist-mono-*.woff2`
- Supporting source artwork: `female-wikipedia-silhouette-group.png` (not currently shipped in `public`)

## Tokens

- Cavendish yellow: `#FFE01B`
- Warm ink: `#241C15`
- Cream surface: `#FBEFE3`
- Paper: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted ink: `#6F6A64`
- Coral accent: `#FF4D74`
- Success: `#2D7B65`
- Error: `#B3263E`

## Typography And Shape

- Display and body: local Geist Sans, with 800-900 weight reserved for primary headings.
- Code and recovery values: local Geist Mono.
- Spacing: 4 / 8 / 16 / 24 / 40 / 64.
- Content panels: 8px radius, 2px warm-ink border.
- Commands: pill shape, at least 44px tall.
- Shadow: `0 4px 12px rgba(36, 28, 21, 0.08)`; key playful surfaces may use a 4-6px ink offset.

## Signature Moves

- Yellow flood-fill for primary moments.
- Wavy coral underline on one primary heading per view.
- Tactile outlined controls with short lift and press feedback.
- Warm, conversational microcopy.
- Status colors appear only when they carry meaning.

## Guardrails

- No pure black, cold blue gradients, glass panels, or purple-pink tech gradients.
- No fake testimonials, metrics, mascots, or illustrations.
- No Mailchimp logo or Freddie likeness.
- Keep dense game boards on a stable grid; decoration must not interfere with scanning or input.
